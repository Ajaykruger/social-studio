## MVP Rules

- Speak to South African nail technicians, salon owners, resellers, and students.
- Keep the voice friendly, professional, practical, and clear.
- Prefer useful salon workflow benefits over vague luxury claims.
- Use product-page or human-approved claims only.
- Do not invent reviews, awards, lab results, testimonials, before-and-after proof, or medical-style claims.
- Every generated post or video must remain draft-only until a human reviewer approves it.

## First Product Focus
