# Crystal Clawz Video Prompt Template

Use this template to generate a short-form product video script for MoneyPrinterTurbo.

## Input

- Product name: {{product_name}}
- Category: {{category}}
- Short description: {{short_description}}
- Key benefits: {{key_benefits}}
- Target customer: {{target_customer}}
- Video angle: {{video_angle}}
- Call to action: {{call_to_action}}
- Platform: {{platform}}

## Prompt

Create a 15 to 30 second vertical video script for Crystal Clawz.

Requirements:
- Format for 9:16 vertical video for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and Shopify product content.
- Use South African Grade 10 English.
- Keep the tone friendly, professional, and focused on nail technicians.
- Make the product benefit clear in the first 3 seconds.
- Include simple visual direction for each scene.
- Include short voiceover lines that are easy to read aloud.
- Include concise on-screen text for each scene.
- End with a strong call to action.

Safety and brand rules:
- Do not make unrealistic claims.
- Do not make medical or health claims.
- Do not invent testimonials, reviews, awards, lab results, or before-and-after proof.
- Do not promise perfect results.
- Do not say the product is suitable for every client unless that is verified.
- Do not mention Shopify, Xero, ERP, posting APIs, or private systems.
- Do not ask for or include API keys.

Output format:
1. Video title
2. Hook
3. Scene-by-scene script with timestamp, visual direction, voiceover, and on-screen text
4. Caption
5. Hashtags
6. CTA
